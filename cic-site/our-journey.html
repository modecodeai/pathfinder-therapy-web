The Pathfinder story

Momentum with purpose.

01

The idea

Pathfinder began with a belief that therapy should feel human, purposeful and grounded in real-world experience.

02

The CIC

Pathfinder Therapy CIC was formed to create community benefit and improve access to meaningful psychological support.

03

The Covenant

Signing the Armed Forces Covenant formalised our commitment to serving personnel, veterans and military families.

04

The team

Therapists and veterans lead Pathfinder with lived understanding, clinical grounding and a shared commitment to recovery.

05

The The Pathfinder Project

The vision is now taking physical shape in Portugal, where future wellbeing and therapeutic programmes can develop.

06

The future

Pathfinder will continue to develop partnerships, outcomes, programmes and access routes for those navigating difficult terrain.
