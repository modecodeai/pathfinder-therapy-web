Our impact pillars

Access. Veterans. Community. Innovation.

Access

Reducing barriers to therapy through affordable, low-cost and referral-based support pathways.

Veterans

Supporting veterans and military families navigating trauma, transition, injury, identity and recovery.

Community

Creating meaningful support for adults in the wider community who need a safe therapeutic space.

Innovation

Developing new approaches including the Pathfinder TERRAIN Pathway™ and the future The Pathfinder Project.
