Privacy Notice

How we use your information

This Privacy Notice explains how Pathfinder Therapy CIC uses information submitted through this website and related enquiries.

Last updated:
June 2026

Controller:
Pathfinder Therapy CIC

Contact:
hello@pathfindertherapy.org.uk

1. Who we are

Pathfinder Therapy CIC is a Community Interest Company registered in England and Wales. Company Number: 17248842. Registered office: Ribble Court, 1 Mead Way, Padiham, Burnley, BB12 7NG.

2. What information we collect

When you use our contact or referral enquiry form, we may collect your name, email address, telephone number, enquiry type, preferred contact method and the message you choose to provide.

Please do not include detailed clinical history, medication information, safeguarding disclosures or urgent risk information in the website form. If further information is needed, we will request it through a more appropriate process.

3. Why we use your information

We use the information you provide to respond to enquiries, consider referral requests, manage partnership or funding enquiries, and keep appropriate records of contact with Pathfinder.

4. Lawful basis

For general website enquiries, we may rely on consent and/or legitimate interests, depending on the nature of the enquiry. If an enquiry includes information relating to health or wellbeing, this may be special category data and will be handled with additional care.

5. Who we share information with

We do not sell personal information. We may share information where necessary with appropriate professionals, service providers, regulators, insurers, legal advisers, safeguarding bodies or emergency services where required by law, safety, safeguarding or professional obligations.

6. Website form processors

The website form uses Cloudflare for hosting, security and bot protection, and Resend to send enquiry emails to Pathfinder. These providers process technical and form-related data for the purpose of delivering the enquiry.

7. Cookies and analytics

Our website uses essential cookies and security tools to operate safely. With your consent, we also use Google Analytics to understand website use and improve our services. You can manage analytics cookie choices using the cookie banner or the Manage Cookies link in the footer. See our Cookie Notice.

8. How long we keep information

General website enquiries will be retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond and manage the enquiry. If an enquiry becomes part of a clinical, referral, safeguarding, contractual or governance process, different retention periods may apply.

9. Your rights

You may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to the use of your personal information, and to complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office if you are unhappy with how your data is handled.

10. Contact us

For privacy questions, email hello@pathfindertherapy.org.uk.

Keeping this notice under review: Pathfinder Therapy CIC will keep this Privacy Notice under review as our services, clinical processes, records management and governance arrangements develop.
