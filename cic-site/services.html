Rocky Ridge

Veteran Trauma Therapy

Specialist therapy for veterans and serving personnel navigating trauma, transition and the impact of military life.

  • Trauma linked to service, injury or operational experience
  • Identity change, transition and loss of role
  • Shame, grief, anger, anxiety and emotional shutdown
  • Working towards stability, meaning and a route forward

Deep Valley

PTSD & Trauma Recovery

Support for people affected by trauma, PTSD symptoms, overwhelm, anxiety, flashbacks, avoidance or feeling stuck in survival mode.

  • Stabilisation and safety-focused work
  • Understanding triggers and trauma responses
  • Working with shame, fear, avoidance and hypervigilance
  • Building emotional regulation and resilience

Forest Path

Counselling & Emotional Support

A confidential space to explore your thoughts, emotions, relationships, patterns and choices at your own pace.

  • Anxiety, depression, grief and stress
  • Relationship and family difficulties
  • Self-understanding and emotional clarity
  • Developing a stronger sense of direction

Men’s Mental Health

Grounded support for men navigating pressure, stress, identity, anger, emotional shutdown, relationship strain or life change.

Urgent help

If you are in crisis

Pathfinder is not an emergency or crisis service.

  • If there is immediate danger to life, call 999 or go to your nearest A&E.
  • For urgent mental health help in England, call NHS 111 and select the mental health option.
  • For someone to talk to at any time, call Samaritans free on 116 123.
  • For confidential text support, text SHOUT to 85258.
