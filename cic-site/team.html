Our Patron

Our Patron

Our Patron supports the mission and public purpose of Pathfinder Therapy CIC, helping us champion better access to informed, compassionate support for the Armed Forces community.

Gregg Stevenson MBE

Patron

Gregg Stevenson MBE is a former Royal Engineer Commando, Paralympic gold medallist and advocate for veterans’ health, rehabilitation and mental wellbeing. As Patron of Pathfinder Therapy CIC, Gregg supports our mission to improve access to compassionate, trauma-informed psychological support for the Armed Forces community and their families.

Standards and accreditations

Organisational Recognition

Pathfinder Therapy CIC is an NCPS Recognised Counselling Service.

Individual team members may hold separate professional registrations, memberships and qualifications. These are listed in their individual profiles where applicable.

NCPS Recognised Counselling Service status applies to Pathfinder Therapy CIC as an organisation and should not be interpreted as confirmation that every individual practitioner holds personal NCPS membership.
