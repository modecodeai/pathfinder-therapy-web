Equality, diversity and inclusion

EDI statement

Pathfinder Therapy CIC aims to provide accessible, respectful and inclusive support.

Applies to:
Clients, staff, contractors, volunteers, partners and visitors.

Contact:
hello@pathfindertherapy.org.uk

Our commitment

Pathfinder Therapy CIC is committed to treating people with dignity, fairness and respect. We aim to create a service culture that recognises difference and reduces barriers to support.

Veterans of all genders

Pathfinder supports veterans of all genders, including women veterans, male veterans, reservists, serving personnel, families and the wider Armed Forces community.

Non-discrimination

We do not discriminate on the basis of age, disability, gender reassignment, marriage or civil partnership, pregnancy or maternity, race, religion or belief, sex, sexual orientation, veteran status, socio-economic background or other protected or relevant characteristics.

Accessibility

We aim to make information and support as accessible as reasonably possible. If you need an adjustment or a different way to communicate with us, please contact us.

Inclusive practice

Therapeutic work should respect the person, their context, their identity and their lived experience. We will continue developing our practice, policies and training as the organisation grows.

Feedback

If you experience a barrier to accessing Pathfinder or have feedback about inclusion and accessibility, contact hello@pathfindertherapy.org.uk.
