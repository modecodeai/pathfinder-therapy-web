Website terms & disclaimer

1. Information only

The information on this website is for general information only. It is not a substitute for professional assessment, therapy, medical advice, legal advice or emergency support.

2. No emergency service

Pathfinder is not an emergency or crisis service. If there is immediate danger to life, call 999 or attend your nearest A&E.

3. No therapeutic relationship through website use

Using this website, submitting a form or sending an enquiry does not create a therapeutic relationship. A therapeutic relationship only begins after appropriate assessment, contracting and acceptance by Pathfinder or an associated clinician.

4. Accuracy and availability

We aim to keep information accurate and up to date, but we cannot guarantee that all information will always be complete, current or uninterrupted.
